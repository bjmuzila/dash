ℹ️ Symbology Cheat Sheet

Deciphering dxFeed indicator formulas & indices

$TICK / $TICK/Q

Compares shares currently ticking up vs ticking down. Readings > +1000 signal major buying programs; < -1000 indicate panic selling.

$TRIN / $TRIN/Q

Arms Index (Adv/Dec volume divided by Adv/Dec issues). A reading below 1.0 is bullish; a reading above 1.0 is bearish.

$ADVN / $DECLN

Straightforward counts of issues trading above or below the prior day's close. Perfect for checking real index participation.

$ADDRL / $ADDRL/Q

Cumulative Advance/Decline Difference. Net cumulative total of advancers minus decliners since market open.

$AVOL / $DVOL / $TVOL

Volume Analysis. Advancing volume, declining volume, and total volume combined. Shows capital intensity directionally.

$HI / $LO

New 52-Week Highs and Lows. Extremes in breakout strength (highs) or capitulation exhaustion (lows).
