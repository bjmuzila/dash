Delta Cliff Reversal
Heavy negative GEX plus a cumulative DEX trough can mark maximum hedge pressure. If downside stalls there, look for a bounce back toward the GEX flip.
GEX Flip Squeeze
A steep DEX slope through the flip means inventory is changing quickly. If signed flow agrees, trade breakout or retest continuation.
Positive Wall Fade
When price reaches a positive GEX wall and DEX flattens into a shelf, avoid chasing and watch for mean reversion.
