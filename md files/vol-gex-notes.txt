Volume GEX target for chart and heatmap:

The goal is the overall net SPX Vol GEX for today, based on all SPX option trades that occurred during the current session.

This should not be derived only from the options chain's aggregated volume field.

Instead, Vol GEX should be calculated from live trade-by-trade flow using:

Tick GEX = TradeDirection x OptionType x Gamma x TradeSize x 50 x SpotPrice^2 x 0.01

Where:
- TradeDirection = +1 for bought at ask, -1 for sold at bid
- OptionType = +1 for calls, -1 for puts
- Gamma = raw Black-Scholes gamma at the moment of the trade
- TradeSize = contracts in that trade
- 50 = ES multiplier
- SpotPrice = ES price at the time of the trade

Then sum all signed tick values across the trading day:

Net Vol GEX = Sum of all Tick GEX values for today

For the chart and heatmap:
- The displayed value should represent today's net signed SPX option flow
- It can be shown by strike, or as an overall net total
- It does not need to be shown as a cumulative running line unless requested
- The important part is that it reflects the net signed flow for today's session

If future edits are made, prefer trade-level signed flow over raw option-chain volume snapshots whenever possible.
